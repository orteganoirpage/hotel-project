Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?+
Check-in is from 3:00 PM. Check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Within 48 hours — one night charge. No-shows are charged the full amount.
Is breakfast included?+
Continental breakfast is included in Executive and Presidential Suite bookings. For other rooms, breakfast can be added for $35 per person per day.
Is there a charge for additional guests?+
Rooms are priced for 2 guests. Each additional guest is charged $20 per night, automatically calculated during booking.
Do you offer airport transfers?+
Yes, private luxury transfers in our Mercedes-Benz fleet. Must be booked 24 hours in advance. Rates from $85 one way.
How do I cancel my reservation?+
Use your confirmation code in the Cancellation section below. Refunds process within 5-7 business days.
